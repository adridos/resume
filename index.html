The Bakery Website

This bakery website emerged from a collaborative effort during a database systems course at Oakland University. Our team embarked on the creation of a user-friendly platform, enabling customers to effortlessly place orders for delectable bakery items scheduled for later pickup. Going beyond the customer interface, we incorporated an employee-centric section where bakery staff could log in and seamlessly manage the order queue.
The backbone of our system is an SQL database meticulously designed to store comprehensive information on the bakery's product inventory, orders in progress, and customer details. For an in-depth understanding of the intricacies involved, the README.md file provides a comprehensive guide to navigating through the functionalities and features of this bakery website.
